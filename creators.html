Frequent questions

How do I Apply?

We only accept creators we deem influential or have the potential to be influential based on their social media profiles.

If you believe you are eligible & you could integrate Sensual Speechinto your content naturally, you can Apply by entering your email above.

How do I receive payouts?

If your application is approved, you can add your PayPal email address from within your Sensual SpeechCreator Dashboard by entering your email above.

We only send payouts through PayPal and payouts are sent at the end of each month.

If you're having any trouble, please contact a representative at Sensual Speechand we can add your PayPal email address manually.

What is the commission structure?

If you're approved, we start you out at 30% recurring commission for anyone who purchases from your link as long as they stay signed up.

We typically offer higher commission over time as you grow as a creator/affiliate for Sensual Speech& setup a generous way for you to eventually earn 45% - 50% commission.

How are conversions approved?

A conversion is approved if it has been pending for at least 30 days first & then processed as approved on the 22nd or 23rd of each month.

Conversions need to remain pending for at least 30 days in order to respect our 30-day money back guarantee on SensualSpeech.com.

Any refunded or disputed payments will be denied commission to ensure we don't pay commission on a payment that SensualSpeech.com did not earn as net revenue.

We also process negative conversion amounts before each payout to cancel any false commission from prorated charges during upgrades as our system doesn't recognize prorated charges right away.

All commission sent is exactly what you're owed & we aim to be as transparent as possible when you are a creator promoting Sensual Speechto your audience.

How can I keep track of my progress?

If your application is accepted, you will be able to fully login to the Sensual SpeechCreator Dashboard by entering your email above via Refersion.

In the dashboard, you can view all important metrics & pending / approved conversions in real-time so you can see how your link is performing.
